# Key Findings

## Overall
- Demand remained stable across the year, but ratings and SLA attainment declined toward year-end.
- A small number of restaurants contributed disproportionately to late orders.

## Customer Behavior
- Retention remained a challenge at ~21% QoQ.
- ~31% of customers placed only one order in Q2.
- Demand peaked during lunch and evening hours, especially on weekends.

## Q4 Operations
- Prep and delivery times increased across many restaurants.
- More orders shifted into late buckets in Q4.
- A few restaurants were major contributors to delays.
